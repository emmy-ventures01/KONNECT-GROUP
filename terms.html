Admin Terms & Conditions

Before applying to be an admin in KONNECT Cruise, please carefully read the rules and responsibilities below. By submitting a request, you agree to abide by these terms.

General Rules for Admins

  • Respect all group members and maintain a positive environment.
  • No spamming, advertising, or posting inappropriate content.
  • Handle conflicts professionally without escalating issues.
  • Follow the instructions and guidance of the group creator, emmyventures.
  • Admins are expected to actively participate and manage the group regularly.
  • Failure to follow rules may result in removal from the admin team.

Responsibilities

  • Approve new members carefully and ensure they fit the group’s vibe.
  • Monitor activity and remove members who violate group rules.
  • Organize and support weekly group activities and games.
  • Keep communication transparent with other admins and emmyventures.
  • Maintain confidentiality of any private admin information.

Agreement

By applying for admin access, you acknowledge that emmyventures has full authority to accept or reject your request. Admins must prioritize the group's harmony and positive energy at all times.
